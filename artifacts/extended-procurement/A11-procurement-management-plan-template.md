Before finalizing this plan, confirm that each element passes the following waste test. These items are static guidance — do not convert to [FIELD: ...] placeholders.

- [ ] Every procurement item has a documented make-or-buy decision with rationale.
- [ ] Contract types are matched to the level of scope definition and risk allocation — not defaulted.
- [ ] Supplier selection criteria are weighted and measurable, not vague preferences.
- [ ] Procurement roles and approval authorities are unambiguous and do not overlap.
- [ ] The procurement schedule is integrated with the project schedule and WBS (A08).
- [ ] Contract administration approach specifies WHO monitors performance and HOW OFTEN.
- [ ] Change order process has defined thresholds and escalation paths.
- [ ] Payment terms align with organizational finance policy and contract milestone structure.
- [ ] Organizational spend thresholds are correctly applied to all items in the register.
- [ ] Procurement risks are cross-referenced to A19 (Risk Register) and are not duplicated without purpose.
