## Definition

A14 is the assembled, approved integration of all subsidiary plans and baselines into a single version-controlled document package. It is the output of SKL-03-01 — Integrate and Align Project Plans. While A06 defines how each management area will be handled, A14 is the final integrated artifact that signals planning is complete and execution may begin.

**Note:** A06 and A14 are closely related. In smaller projects, they may be the same document. In larger or more complex projects, A06 contains the subsidiary plans while A14 is the formal integrated baseline package signed off by the sponsor.

**PMI Source:** PMBOK8 Guide §2.1.6.2; Process-Groups §3.3.

## Purpose

- Signal formal planning completion and readiness for execution
- Provide the integrated reference that all execution and monitoring skills operate against
- Record sponsor/governance approval of the full plan and baselines
- Serve as the baseline document for all integrated change control
