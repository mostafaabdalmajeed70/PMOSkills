## 1. PMO Typology Overview

Project Management Offices are not monolithic. Under the **PMI PMO Practice Guide**, a PMO is tailored to match the specific organizational hierarchy, authority levels, delivery methods, and strategic objectives of the enterprise. These PMO archetypes are classified across four dimensions: **Oversight Scope, Control Level, Strategic Focus, and Lifecycle Span**.

---

## 2. Core PMO Archetypes (By Control & Authority)

The three primary structural types defined in PMBOK 8 indicate the level of control the PMO exerts over project execution:

| Archetype | Control level | Scope & Primary Authority | Ideal Use Case |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Supportive PMO** | **Low** | Consultative. Provides templates, best practices, training repositories, and lessons learned. PMs operate autonomously. | Mature organizations with highly skilled, self-managing project managers. |
| **Controlling PMO** | **Medium** | Compliance-oriented. Mandates specific methodologies, frameworks, baseline checks, and PMIS usage. Audits conformance. | Organizations in highly regulated fields (finance, healthcare, aerospace). |
| **Directive PMO** | **High** | Direct management. PMs report directly to the PMO. The PMO owns and executes the project portfolio. | Large enterprises executing high-risk, strategically critical transformations. |

## 3. Advanced & Specialty PMO Types (20+ Archetypes)

Modern enterprises combine multiple PMO styles to create specialized governance hubs:

### 3.1 Strategic & Portfolio PMOs (Strategic Focus)
1. **Enterprise PMO (EPMO):** Aligns all corporate project initiatives with CEO-level corporate strategy and finance boundaries.
2. **Portfolio PMO:** Manages prioritizing, resource modeling, and investment limits across a single corporate portfolio.
3. **Strategic PMO:** Evaluates macro-market dynamics, corporate acquisitions, and innovation investments.
4. **Digital PMO:** Directly governs enterprise digital transformation, cloud migrations, and tech-stack modernizations.

### 3.2 Program & Project PMOs (Operational Focus)
5. **Program PMO:** Governs a set of related, interdependent projects to maximize benefits realization.
6. **Project-Specific PMO:** Instantiated for a single, massive mega-project (e.g., $100M+ infrastructure development) and decommissioned upon closeout.
7. **Business Unit / Divisional PMO:** Located within a specific business division (e.g., Marketing, HR) to handle localized initiatives.
8. **Operational PMO:** Supports continuous improvement, customer service excellence, and product updates.

### 3.3 Delivery Method Adaptations (Agile & Hybrid Focus)
9. **Value Delivery Office (VDO):** Focuses on value streams, agile coaching, and scrum-of-scrums coordination.
10. **Center of Excellence (CoE) PMO:** Serves as a coaching hub for professional project skills, agile tooling, and standard audits.
11. **Hybrid PMO:** Governs blended environments where some teams use waterfall and others use agile sprints.
12. **Virtual PMO:** A decentralized PMO utilizing cloud-based PMIS and asynchronous tools to support globally remote teams.

### 3.4 Sourcing & External Adaptations (Commercial Focus)
13. **Customer-Facing PMO:** Governs professional services delivered directly to paying clients, managing client SLAs.
14. **Vendor / Procurement PMO:** Administers third-party supplier agreements, contracts, and software licenses.
15. **Shared Services PMO:** Centralized hub sharing technical experts (e.g., QA testers, DBAs) across internal teams.
16. **Temporary / Ad-hoc PMO:** Formed rapidly to manage emergency initiatives (e.g., rapid pandemic response setups) and closed post-crisis.

### 3.5 Specialized Function PMOs (Governance Focus)
17. **Governance PMO:** Focuses exclusively on regulatory compliance, risk registers, and steering audits.
18. **IT PMO:** Governs technical infrastructure deployments, cybersecurity updates, and internal help desks.
19. **Regional / Geographic PMO:** Instantiated to manage projects in a specific country or region, aligning with local labor laws.
20. **Analytics & BI PMO:** Specializes in real-time project metrics, EVM calculations, and executive dashboards.

## 4. Scenario Integration (Meridian CRM System Upgrade)

The *Meridian CRM System Upgrade* utilized a **Divisional IT PMO** backed by the strategic governance of the **EPMO**:
* **Divisional IT PMO Role:** Directly managed developer resources, monitored daily sprint velocity, and tracked the schedule baseline (`PR12`).
* **EPMO Role:** Provided the standard change control guidelines (`PR31`), reviewed the financial cost baseline (`PR16`), and audited regulatory compliance with privacy laws.
* **Outcome:** Ensured seamless coordination between daily technical tasks and executive business strategies.
