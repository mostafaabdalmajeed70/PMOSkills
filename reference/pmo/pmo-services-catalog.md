## 1. Introduction

This catalog maps the **26 standard PMO services** defined in the [PMO Standard Services Matrix](./pmo-services.md) to the corresponding executable skills in the PMOSkills repository. This alignment ensures that AI agents and PMO practitioners can directly trace operational services to the structured methods, templates, and validation criteria provided by this operating system.
