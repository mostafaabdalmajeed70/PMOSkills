## Overview

PMBOK 8 defines **12 principles** in The Standard for Project Management (§3). These principles are not prescriptive rules — they are behavioral guideposts that inform how project practitioners act, decide, and lead across all projects regardless of lifecycle approach.

Every skill in this repository embeds the relevant principles in its design. This index maps all 12 principles and their cross-references.
