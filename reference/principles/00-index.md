# Principles Index
**Type:** Reference Index
**PMBOK8 Source:** PMBOK8 Standard §3 — Project Management Principles
**Version:** 1.0.0

## Purpose

The PMBOK 8 Standard defines 12 principles that describe the foundational values and behaviors expected of project practitioners. Principles are not prescriptive methods or processes — they are the ethical and professional foundation from which methods, approaches, and practices are derived.

These 12 principles apply regardless of development approach (predictive, adaptive, or hybrid), industry sector, or project size. They complement and reinforce each other; no single principle operates in isolation.

## The 12 Principles

| ID | Principle | PMBOK8 Source | Brief Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| P01 | Be a Diligent, Respectful, and Caring Steward | §3.1 | Act with integrity and care for organizational, social, and environmental resources |
| P02 | Create a Collaborative Project Team Environment | §3.2 | Build team conditions — shared purpose, clear roles, psychological safety, and mutual respect |
| P03 | Effectively Engage with Stakeholders | §3.3 | Proactively understand and engage stakeholder interests, needs, and concerns throughout the lifecycle |
| P04 | Focus on Value | §3.4 | Maintain consistent focus on intended value — financial, social, environmental, or strategic |
| P05 | Recognize, Evaluate, and Respond to System Interactions | §3.5 | Understand projects as part of larger systems with complex interdependencies |
| P06 | Demonstrate Leadership Behaviors | §3.6 | Lead at all levels — motivate, communicate, model behaviors, and make accountable decisions |
| P07 | Tailor Based on Context | §3.7 | Deliberately select, adapt, and combine approaches to fit the project's unique context |
| P08 | Build Quality into Processes and Deliverables | §3.8 | Integrate quality prevention into processes — not just inspection at delivery |
| P09 | Navigate Complexity | §3.9 | Recognize, anticipate, and respond to complexity through judgment and adaptive sense-making |
| P10 | Optimize Risk Responses | §3.10 | Identify and manage both threats and opportunities with proportionate, cost-effective responses |
| P11 | Embrace Adaptability and Resiliency | §3.11 | Build capacity to respond to change and absorb disruption without losing project purpose |
| P12 | Enable Change to Achieve the Envisioned Future State | §3.12 | Prepare organizations and stakeholders for transition and sustain change after delivery |
