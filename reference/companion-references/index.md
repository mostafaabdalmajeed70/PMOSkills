## Purpose

This index serves as the **central directory mapping the 24 official PMI companion references** to the core PMBOK 8th Edition Performance Domains and the skill layers in the PMOSkills repository. It enables practitioners to easily navigate the secondary literature and practice standards recommended by PMI for advanced project management execution.

## Mappings to PMBOK 8 Performance Domains

Each companion reference is mapped to a core domain in the PMBOK 8th Edition ecosystem:

### 1. Stakeholders Performance Domain
* **Primary References:** `[PMO-Guide]`, `[Lexicon]`
* **Associated Processes:** PR02 (Identify Stakeholders), PR27 (Plan Stakeholder Engagement)
* **Description:** Details stakeholder identification strategies, power grids, and engagement matrixes.

### 2. Team Performance Domain
* **Primary References:** `[PMO-Guide]`, `[Lexicon]`
* **Associated Processes:** PR18 (Plan Resource Management), PR19 (Estimate Activity Resources)
* **Description:** Covers team behaviors, organization structures, and training competency models.

### 3. Development Approach and Life Cycle Domain
* **Primary References:** `[APG]`, `[DAD-Intro]`
* **Associated Processes:** PR04 (Plan Scope Management), PR08 (Plan Schedule Management)
* **Description:** Essential practice guidelines for Agile, Hybrid, predictive lifecycles, and agile tailoring.

### 4. Planning Performance Domain
* **Primary References:** `[Scheduling-3E]`, `[WBS-3E]`, `[Requirements]`, `[BAP-2E]`
* **Associated Processes:** PR03 (Develop PMP), PR05–PR17 (All planning processes)
* **Description:** Outlines advanced requirements elicitation, schedule modeling, WBS decomposition, and estimating formulas.

### 5. Project Work Performance Domain
* **Primary References:** `[PMO-Guide]`, `[Change-Guide]`
* **Associated Processes:** PR28 (Direct & Manage Work), PR29 (Manage Knowledge)
* **Description:** Details operations management, CCB procedures, lessons learned, and knowledge repositories.

### 6. Delivery Performance Domain
* **Primary References:** `[Forms-Book]`, `[Lexicon]`
* **Associated Processes:** PR32 (Validate Scope), PR40 (Close Project)
* **Description:** Details final transition plans, product audits, and client acceptance procedures.

### 7. Measurement Performance Domain
* **Primary References:** `[EVM]`
* **Associated Processes:** PR30 (Monitor & Control Work), PR34 (Control Schedule), PR35 (Control Costs), PR37 (Control Quality)
* **Description:** The definitive EVM framework detailing CV, SV, SPI, CPI, EAC, and ETC metrics and variance analysis.

### 8. Uncertainty Performance Domain
* **Primary References:** `[Risk-PPP]`, `[Complexity]`
* **Associated Processes:** PR21 (Plan Risk Management), PR22 (Identify Risks), PR39 (Monitor Risks)
* **Description:** Analyzes qualitative and quantitative risk analysis, risk response strategies, complexity indices, and Monte Carlo modeling.
