# PR20 — Plan Communications Management

## Classification
- **Performance Domain:** Stakeholders
- **Process Group:** Planning
- **PMBOK 8 Reference:** Section 2.5 (Stakeholders Performance Domain)
- **Process Groups Practice Guide Reference:** Section 5.17 (implied)

---

## Purpose
Plan Communications Management is the process of developing an appropriate approach and plan for project communications activities based on the information needs of each stakeholder or group, available organizational assets, and the needs of the project. The key benefit is a documented approach to engage stakeholders effectively and efficiently by presenting relevant information in a timely manner.

---

## Inputs
- Project charter
- Project management plan (resource management plan, stakeholder engagement plan)
- Project documents: requirements documentation, stakeholder register
- Enterprise environmental factors
- Organizational process assets

---

## Tools & Techniques
- Expert judgment
- Communication requirements analysis
- Communication technology
- Communication models
- Communication methods
- Interpersonal and team skills (communication styles assessment, political awareness, cultural awareness)
- Data representation (stakeholder engagement assessment matrix)
- Meetings

---

## Outputs
- Communications management plan
- Project management plan updates (stakeholder engagement plan)
- Project documents updates (project schedule, stakeholder register)

---

## Key Concepts
- The communications management plan describes: stakeholder communication requirements, information to be communicated (format, content, language, level of detail), escalation processes, time frame and frequency, responsible parties, methods/technologies, and constraints.
- Communication dimensions: interactive (multi-directional), push (one-way), pull (recipient-initiated).
- Communication styles assessment is used especially for unsupportive stakeholders.
- Cultural and language diversity require tailored communication approaches.
- Effective communication underpins stakeholder engagement across all performance domains.
