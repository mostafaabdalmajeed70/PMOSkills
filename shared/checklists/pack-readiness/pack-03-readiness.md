## Purpose

This checklist verifies that the detailed planning processes are complete, baseline integrations have been checked, and the overall Project Management Plan (A14) has been formally approved and baselined before execution (Pack 04) begins.

---

---

## Gate Transition Criteria

*   **PASS (8 / 8 met):** Full integrated baseline established and approved. Ready to enter **Pack 04 (Executing)**.
*   **WARN (6 / 8 met):** Minor tailoring or sustainability details pending, but core baselines (Scope, Schedule, Cost) are complete. **Condition:** Sponsor sign-off on partial baseline.
*   **FAIL (< 6 met OR any core baseline missing):** Transition rejected. Scope, Schedule, or Cost is unapproved.
