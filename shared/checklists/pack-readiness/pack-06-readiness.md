## Purpose

This checklist verifies that the project closure criteria have been successfully met, business value benefits transition plans are signed, and sustainability decommissioning/waste diversion targets have been archived before final release.

---

## Readiness Checklist

| Category | Check Item | Target Skill / Reference | Verified Status |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Acceptance** | [ ] All deliverables are formally validated and signed off by the Customer. | `SKL-05-09` / `A38` | [ ] Yes / [ ] No |
| **Handovers** | [ ] Operations and Benefits Transition Record (A24) is approved by Business Owner. | `SKL-06-01` / `A24` | [ ] Yes / [ ] No |
| **Financials** | [ ] All project billing contracts, purchase orders, and supplier accounts are settled. | `SKL-06-01` / `A16` | [ ] Yes / [ ] No |
| **ESG** | [ ] Final circular economy diversion rate and decommissioning metrics are logged. | `A-NEW-SUST` | [ ] Yes / [ ] No |
| **Archiving** | [ ] Project lessons learned (A30) and configuration records (A34) are archived. | `A30` / `A34` | [ ] Yes / [ ] No |

---

## Gate Transition Criteria

*   **PASS (5 / 5 met):** Project is ready for formal close-out.
*   **WARN (4 / 5 met):** Minor supplier final invoices are pending, but all client handovers are approved. **Condition:** Action plan to settle in 10 days.
*   **FAIL (< 4 met OR deliverables not signed):** Cannot close the project. Work remains unaccepted.
